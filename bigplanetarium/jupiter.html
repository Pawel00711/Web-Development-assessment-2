About Jupiter

Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System. It is a gas giant made mostly of hydrogen and helium.

Known for its Great Red Spot, a giant storm bigger than Earth, Jupiter has dozens of moons and a faint ring system.
